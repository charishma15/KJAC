Fully Licensed and Certified for Your Peace of Mind

At KJAC Consulting LLC, we are a NYC Department of Buildings registered Class-2 Special Inspection Agency (License #007961), led by a licensed Professional Engineer (License #092580). Your project’s compliance and safety are in expert hands.

  • Certified NYC DOB Special Inspection Agency.
  • Inspections fully compliant with NYC Building Codes
  • Adherence to OSHA, DEP, SCA, DOT, and FDNY standards

We represent the best interests of our clients, ensuring every construction project meets official regulatory requirements and building code specifications.

Expert MEP Design and Engineering Solutions

Our MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) engineering services ensure efficient, sustainable, and code-compliant systems integrated into your building's overall design.

  • Mechanical HVAC system design and consultation
  • Power distribution and electrical system engineering
  • Plumbing system layout in compliance with NYC codes
  • Code analysis and permit documentation assistance

Full-Scope Special Inspections Across Project Phases

Our comprehensive inspection services guarantee that your project construction complies with DOB Chapter 17, BC 1704, BC 1705, and all applicable laws.

  • Flood Zone Compliance and Fuel-Oil Piping Inspections
  • Sprayed Fire-Resistant Materials and Structural Steel Inspections
  • Mechanical, Plumbing, Fire Protection Systems Inspections
  • Smoke Control Systems and Seismic Isolation Inspections

We help identify and resolve compliance issues early, saving clients from costly fines, stop-work orders, and delays.

Site Safety Excellence for Reliable Project Delivery

KJAC Consulting LLC ensures that all construction activities maintain the highest safety standards in accordance with NYC DOB Chapter 33 and OSHA guidelines.

  • Site Safety Plans (SSP) customized for each project
  • Site Safety Managers (SSM) for daily site operations
  • Full Construction Safety Audits and Hazard Assessments
  • Development of customized Hazard and Safety Programs (HASP)
