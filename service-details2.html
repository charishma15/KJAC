Ensuring Compliance, Protection, and Peace of Mind On Every Site

KJAC Consulting LLC specializes in Construction Site Safety Management, providing certified personnel and strategies that align with NYC DOB regulations and BC Chapter 33. From certified Site Safety Managers (SSM) and Coordinators (SSC) to detailed SSP and HASP plans, we mitigate risk while streamlining site operations.

  • Preparation and filing of Site Safety Plans (SSP) and Health and Safety Plans (HASP).
  • Certified Site Safety Manager (SSM), Coordinator (SSC), and Construction Superintendent (CS) services.
  • Regular site safety audits and DOB inspection readiness checks.
  • Development of safety egress plans, signage, and hazard control protocols.
  • Full compliance with NYC Construction Codes, OSHA, and DOB Chapter 33.

Whether you're managing a major high-rise or a renovation project, our experienced professionals ensure your site remains compliant, your team protected, and your timeline uninterrupted.

Trust KJAC Consulting LLC to provide proactive, qualified site safety solutions that safeguard workers, the public, and your project's success.
