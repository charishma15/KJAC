Strategic Site Safety Plans for Project Readiness and DOB Approval

KJAC Consulting LLC develops and manages Site Safety Plans (SSPs) that fulfill NYC DOB Chapter 33 requirements. Whether your project requires a Superintendent, SSM, or SSC, we create clear, code-aligned safety documentation to guide your operations and support permit acquisition.

  • Custom DOB-compliant Site Safety Plans tailored to each phase of construction.
  • Includes drawings, signage plans, egress layouts, and material handling protocols.
  • We coordinate directly with DOB and other agencies to facilitate smooth approval.
  • We handle SSP updates and resubmissions as jobsite conditions evolve.
  • Fully aligned with OSHA, FDNY, and DOT safety compliance standards.

Our team ensures your SSP is not just a document — it's a real-time, usable guide for field teams and regulators alike.

From project kickoff to final sign-off, trust KJAC Consulting LLC to deliver site safety planning that reduces risk, accelerates permits, and ensures legal compliance.
