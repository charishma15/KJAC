Integrated MEP Engineering Solutions Aligned with NYC Code and Performance Standards

KJAC Consulting LLC offers full-spectrum MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) engineering services tailored to your project’s size and complexity. From system design and code analysis to performance optimization and field coordination, our team ensures safety, sustainability, and compliance across all MEP components.

  • HVAC load analysis, duct design, and mechanical equipment selection aligned with NYC Building Code.
  • Power distribution layouts, lighting, and emergency system designs meeting NEC and FDNY standards.
  • Plumbing layouts for water, sanitary, and storm systems with DOB-approved backflow prevention and pipe sizing.
  • Integration of fire alarms, low-voltage, and smart control systems in new construction and retrofit projects.
  • Professional support for DOB submissions, TR1/TR8 energy compliance, and coordination with other trades.

With licensed Professional Engineers and hands-on field knowledge, we help you design efficient, code-compliant systems while minimizing delays and rework. Our precision-first approach improves performance, safety, and approval outcomes.

Choose KJAC Consulting LLC for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing engineering that supports your construction goals — from concept to certification.
